Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can you start on my project?

I typically start new projects within 1-2 weeks of finalizing agreements. For smaller projects or current clients, I can sometimes accommodate faster turnarounds. My schedule is updated monthly, so please inquire about current availability.

What does your content creation process look like?

My process involves four key steps: 1) Thorough research of your industry, competitors, and target keywords, 2) Developing a detailed outline for your approval, 3) Writing high-quality, SEO-optimized content, and 4) Revisions based on your feedback. Each piece goes through multiple edits to ensure it meets the highest standards.

How do you ensure the content will rank on Google?

While no one can guarantee specific rankings (beware of those who do!), my approach combines careful keyword research, search intent analysis, and SEO best practices. I analyze top-ranking content in your niche to identify patterns and opportunities, then create comprehensive content that addresses user needs better than what's currently ranking. My track record of first-page rankings demonstrates the effectiveness of this approach.

Do you offer revisions for your content?

Yes, all content packages include two rounds of revisions at no additional cost. I believe in getting your content right and ensuring you're completely satisfied. Additional revision rounds can be arranged if needed. My goal is to deliver content that perfectly aligns with your brand voice and strategic objectives.

How do you handle complex technical topics?

I specialize in translating complex technical concepts into clear, accessible content. My approach involves thorough research, consulting with subject matter experts when needed, and breaking down complicated ideas into digestible sections. For highly specialized topics, I'll work closely with your team to ensure technical accuracy while maintaining readability for your target audience.
